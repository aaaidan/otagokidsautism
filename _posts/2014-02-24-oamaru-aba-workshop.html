The Waitaki Trust is running an ABA workshop next month.

Thursday 20th March, 10am to 3pm

Moeraki room at The Brydone Hotel

$30 per person

If you are interested in attending, please contact someone from the Waitaki Trust directly. It will be especially beneficial for families new to ABA therapy or who have recently had their child diagnosed with ASD.

Covered in the workshop:

• ISADD structure and therapy

• Individualised programmes

• What is Autism

• Early signs

• Child development

• Causes of autism

• ABA and research

• Compic system for language
